Only CHAR CRUST® Seals in the Juices®

Our original Char Crust® dry-rub seasoning was created at our family’s legendary Chicago steakhouse in 1957. We dredged prime dry-aged steaks in Char Crust® and cooked them to juicy perfection. Our seasonings seared beautifully and created their signature Char Crust® that Seals in the Juices®.

Since then, we’ve added other delicious rubs and specialty salts to our line, but we still make all of our blends ourselves, using premium, kosher, clean-label ingredients. You can use our dry rubs on any meat or fish... or veggies. Watch our videos or try our rubs for yourself and see how only Char Crust® Seals in the Juices!
