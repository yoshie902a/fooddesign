Recipes & Cooking Methods

Keep it simple!

Whether it’s your first time or 100th time using Char Crust® rubs, you can follow one of the easy cooking methods below and simply broil, bake, grill, pan-sear, sauté or deep-fry to perfection. No recipe needed!

Cooking Method #1 of 5

BROIL

  • Preheat broiler and broilng pan.
  • Place rack near the top of your oven. You’ll want your meat or fish to be no futher than 2 or 3 inches from the heating element.
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • If cooking a low-fat protein (seafood or bone- less skinless chicken) drizzle or spray cooking oil over the surface of the seasoned protein.
  • Flip protein every 1 - 2 minutes during cooking to ensure even cooking and no burning.

Cooking Method #2 of 5

BAKE/ROAST

  • Place sheet pan or roasting pan in oven
  • Preheat oven to 450 degrees
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • Drizzle or spray surface of seasoned protein with canola oil or other oil suitable for high-heat.
  • Place meat or fish on baking/roasting pan.
  • Cook for 10 minutes, then turn oven temperature down to 350 degrees
  • Continue cooking until desired level of doneness is reached

See Video >

Cooking Method #3 of 5

BROIL

  • Preheat one side of grill to high (450 – 550 degrees), and the other side to medium low(about 350 degrees)
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • Drizzle or spray surface of seasoned protein with canola oil or other oil suitable for high-heat cooking
  • Place meat or fish on high heat side of grill
  • Cook for 2 minutes, then flip and cook for 2 minutes on the other side
  • Move protein to the medium-low side of the grill
  • Flip protein every 2 minutes during cooking to ensure even cooking and no burning
  • Continue cooking until desired level of doneness is reached

See Video >

Cooking Method #4 of 5

PAN-SEAR

  • Preheat cast-iron pan to medium-high (400 - 425 degrees)
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • Drizzle or spray surface of seasoned protein with canola oil or other oil suitable for high-heat cooking
  • Place meat or fish inpan.
  • Cook for 2 minutes, then flip and cook for 2 minutes on the other side
  • Flip protein every 2 minutes during cooking to ensure even cooking and no burning
  • Continue cooking until desired level of doneness is reached

See Video >

Cooking Method #5 of 5

GEORGE FOREMAN® GRILL

  • Preheat grill
  • Dredge meat or fish in Char Crust just before cooking
  • Place meat or fish on grill, and close lid
  • Cook for 2 minutes, then flip and cook for 2 minutes on the other side
  • Flip protein every 2 minutes during cooking to ensure even cooking and no burning
  • Continue cooking until desired level of doneness is reached
